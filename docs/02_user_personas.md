# User Personas

## Persona 1 – Operations Manager

### Role
Operations Manager

### Responsibilities
- Monitor daily healthcare operations and ensure smooth execution of processes.
- Track Turnaround Time (TAT) and Service Level Agreement (SLA) compliance.
- Monitor pending approvals and operational escalations.
- Review daily productivity and team performance.

### Daily Problems
- Frequently switches between multiple dashboards to monitor different operational processes.
- Checks emails for approvals and important updates.
- Monitors WhatsApp groups for escalations and operational changes.
- Is not highly technical and occasionally forgets Excel formulas or data analysis techniques needed for operational reporting.
- Spends significant time gathering information before making decisions.

### Goals
Reduce the time spent switching between multiple tools and provide a centralized view of operational insights so that decisions can be made quickly and confidently.

### How the AI Copilot Helps
- Provides a daily operational summary through email, Slack, or the application dashboard.
- Highlights critical issues such as SLA breaches, pending approvals, and high-priority escalations.
- Explains the reason behind recommendations using an "Explain" feature.
- Suggests next-best actions to help prioritize operational tasks.

## Persona 2 – Support Executive

### Role
Support Executive

### Responsibilities
- Handle customer queries through calls, emails, and chat.
- Resolve operational issues across multiple healthcare processes.
- Coordinate with internal teams whenever an issue requires escalation.

### Daily Problems
- Each healthcare process has different workflows, partners, dashboards, and resolution procedures.
- It is difficult to remember every process, policy, and troubleshooting step.
- Support executives often spend time searching documentation or contacting other teams for information.
- Escalations are sometimes delayed because it is unclear which team owns a particular issue.

### Goals
Provide instant access to process knowledge, issue resolution steps, and customer-specific information so that support executives can resolve queries faster and with greater confidence.

### How the AI Copilot Helps
- Answers questions related to healthcare processes, SOPs, and operational guidelines.
- Retrieves relevant information for a specific Order ID, Patient ID, or case when applicable.
- Guides the support executive through issue resolution.
- Recommends the correct team for escalation and assists in initiating the escalation workflow when necessary.
