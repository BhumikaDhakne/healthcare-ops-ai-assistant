# Product Name

Healthcare Operations AI Assistant

---

# Vision

Enable healthcare operations and support teams to work more efficiently by providing a single AI-powered workspace that answers operational questions, retrieves relevant information, summarizes daily operational updates, and recommends the next best actions without requiring users to switch between multiple systems.

---

# Target Users

- Operations Team
- Operations Managers
- Support Executives
- Care Coordinators

---

# Problem Statement

Healthcare operations teams rely on multiple dashboards, SOP documents, emails, spreadsheets, and communication channels to perform their daily work. This constant context switching makes it difficult to find information quickly, resolve customer issues efficiently, monitor operational performance, and make informed decisions.

---

# Success Criteria

The MVP will be considered successful if it can:

- Reduce the need to switch between multiple operational tools.
- Help users find information faster.
- Improve operational decision-making through AI-generated recommendations.
- Reduce the time required to resolve common operational queries.
- Serve as a centralized AI workspace for healthcare operations.
